Redwall Home - I am that is, my sword shall wield for me.
The Redwall fantasy saga follows the adventures and life and death struggles of an eclectic group of anthropomorphic animals living-in Redwall Abbey, located in Mossflower Woods.
The Jacques novels span the many pivotal periods in the storied history of Redwall. Due to the widely-spaced storylines, few characters span one novel to the next. The exception is Martin the Warrior, founder of Redwall Abbey, whose adventures and heroism inspire later Redwall denizens, often as a spiritual vision. The sword of Martin the Warrior is believed by many characters to be magical, thus the tagline "I am that is, my sword shall wield for me."
Although the main setting is an abbey, and several characters are referred to as monks and friars, the series is nearly devoid of religious beliefs or practices.
